    We are committed to promoting board game culture in China.

    Board games are our main business, and we bring outstanding games from all over the world to Chinese players. At the same time, we are also committed to introducing China's excellent original Board games to the world.

    Design

    From prototype design to art design, from numerical balance to cost control, we have professionals handling every step of board game design.

    Agency

    While bringing outstanding games from all over the world to Chinese players, we are also committed to introducing China's excellent original Board games to the world.

    Operation

    Whether crowdfunding campaign plans, copywriting, art design, management, promotion, or overall schemes, as well as retail packaging, publicity, copywriting, and art design, they all fall within our business scope.

    Sales

    We have a convenient logistics network that supports online distribution, and we also have a consortium of various board game stores that provides offline consignment and sales services.
